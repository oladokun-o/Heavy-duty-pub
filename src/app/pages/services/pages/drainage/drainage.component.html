DRAIN DESILTING AND CONSTRUCTION

A lot of road failures are attributed to ponding and flooding due to the inability of the drains to contain the water flow passing through. Many drain walls collapse because of constant soaking of the walls caused by stagnant water. Majority of the drain have been blocked due to waste bags and plastics piling up in the drains. We offer very effective clearing and cleaning of the drains and carting away. We do it at a very reasonable cost. Drains can also be re constructed to continue to serve it purpose.
